Carpools

All carpool reimbursements have to be pre-approved.

A carpool consists of 3 or more students intending to participate in Pearl Hacks.

Once you receive registration information, e-mail us at hello@pearlhacks.com with subject line "Carpool Reimbursement". List the names of participants who will carpool together and which schools, city and state you will be traveling from. You may be asked to submit reimbursement documents prior to or after the event in order to receive your travel grants. Qualifications for carpool grants are as follows:

  • RSVP via link in your email
  • Drive yourself and at least 2 passengers to Pearl Hacks
  • Make sure everyone checks in (even if you have to come late)
  • Submit a project

We cannot make exceptions to this policy. Limit one per carpool.

Charter Buses

We will be offering buses this year! We will have 3 buses that will be picking people up in Florida, Georiga, Virginia, Maryland, Pennsylvania, New Jersey and New York. Specific stops and more information will be out once routes are finalized. Bus signup emails will be sent out to eligible participants around mid-January.

Flights

We will be offering flight reimbursements on a first come first serve basis with the amount reimbursed dependent on where you are traveling from. If you are traveling from the West Coast or Canada, the maximum amount we are able to reimburse will be $400. If you are traveling from anywhere else in the US, the maximum amount we are able to reimburse will be $300. We are not able to offer flight reimbursements to participants from places that we are sending a bus to. Lastly, we will be sending emails out weekly to notify participants of their eligibility for flight reimbursements so if you've already signed up, be on the lookout for that email! If you have any questions about the process, please email us at hello@pearlhacks.com

International Travel

We love international participants! Unfortunately other than Canada, Pearl Hacks cannot provide assistance for international travel or visa paperwork. However, if you do make it to Pearl Hacks, send us an e-mail with subject line: "International Participant". The transportation team would love to show you around Chapel Hill if time permits!
